Looking into the Popularity of Animal Crossing: New Horizons

Stephanie Grasso

What is this?

Final Project for Data Visualization that will look into the rise of popularity in the Nintendo game title Animal Crossing New Horizons. Specifically the traction it's gained throughout the years, the impact of sales, and how the audience responds to the many different animals the game has to offer.

> Alpha Report

Taking data from Google search trends we can see the volume of people's search of "Animal Crossing" throughout time. From the visualization we can see how the search activity spikes around the same time a new game is launched. In comparison to previous games, we can observe the significant spike in search trends around the time the New Horizons game was released around March 2020.

Besides an increase in search volume, New Horizons has also shown it's title success through its revenue in sales. Looking at how New Horizons compares to other Animal Crossing titles, we can see how New Horizons was able to make more than double the sales from New Leaf.

One of the game's charm points lies within the many characters included in the game for the player to interact with and live on their island with them. With many available there are certain villagers that stand above the rest and it's apparent given the attention they acquire from player communities. In this visualization we look at the estimated search volume of every villager and which one's are more popular than others.
